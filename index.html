Our Mission

At SeEars, we're dedicated to developing cutting-edge aural interaction methods that make technology more accessible and engaging. Our primary focus is on creating solutions for blind and visually impaired users, but our innovations extend to various applications where sound can enhance user experience.

Our Work

Accessibility Solutions

Developing sonification and environment auralization methods specifically designed for blind users, making digital content more accessible.

Gaming Audio

Exploring innovative audio designs for games that create immersive experiences through sound.

Media Accessibility

Creating automatic aural descriptions for photos and films, making visual content accessible through sound.
